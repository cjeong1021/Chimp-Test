The game will display numbers in boxes and the numbers must be clicked in the order shown (1,2,3,4,etc...) Once the first number is clicked, all the other numbers will disappear and they will have to be clicked from memory.

The faster you complete a level, the more score you will get. Think carefully if you want to rush to get the max points, or take your time and don't waste your tries.

The Chimpanzee test is a test on working memory. Studies have shown that Chimpanzees outperform humans on average, and are able to memorize over 9 digits 90% of the time!
